This is my first ever Terraria mod.

This mod adds a variety of new items, weapons, and accessories to enhance your gameplay experience.

All items were recommended by and created for friends. They are designed to be fun and (not very) balanced additions to the game.

This mod has calamity support, meaning that if you have the Calamity Mod installed, you will be able to use the items from this mod alongside the items from Calamity.

Mod is currently in development, and more items will be added in future updates. Please feel free to provide feedback and suggestions for new items or improvements to existing ones.
